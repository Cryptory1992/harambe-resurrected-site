About Harambe Resurrected

By harnessing the power of AI, we are resurrecting Harambe. Harambe Resurrected is more than just a meme coin; it's a movement. Inspired by the legendary Harambe, our mission is to honor his legacy and bring the community together through innovation, fun, and purpose.

Our vision also extends to making a real-world impact. A significant part of our mission includes partnering with wildlife conservation organizations. By donating a portion of transaction fees to support gorilla habitats, we aim to contribute to preserving Harambe's kind and ensuring a sustainable future for them.
