About Harambe Resurrected

By harnessing the power of AI, we are resurrecting Harambe. Harambe Resurrected is more than just a meme coin; it's a movement. Inspired by the legendary Harambe, our mission is to honor his legacy and bring the community together through innovation, fun, and purpose. With Harambe Resurrected, we aim to build a thriving ecosystem that combines the power of blockchain technology, community engagement, and charitable initiatives.

Our vision is to create a project that not only entertains but also makes a real-world impact. From launching a unique AI-powered chatbot to supporting wildlife conservation efforts, Harambe Resurrected is here to make a difference. Join us on this exciting journey as we unite meme culture with meaningful utility.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Project Foundation
    • Research & Development
      • Finalize tokenomics (supply, tax, etc.).
      • Develop the Harambe AI chatbot for Telegram.
    • Community Building
      • Launch Telegram and Twitter accounts.
      • Begin building hype with Harambe-themed memes, trivia, and teasers.
  • Phase 2: Token Launch
    • Mainnet Deployment
      • Launch $HARAMBES on Solana mainnet.
      • Add liquidity on Raydium.
    • Initial Marketing Push
      • Launch meme campaigns and influencer collaborations.
      • Host giveaways and community challenges.
  • Phase 3: Utility Development
    • AI Harambe Chatbot (Core Utility)
      • Add features like market updates, Harambe memes, and community Q&A.
      • Gamify the bot (e.g., trivia games where users earn $HARAMBES).
    • Jungle NFT Series
      • Launch a collection of Harambe-themed NFTs with perks for holders.
      • Rare NFTs could unlock lower transaction fees or staking rewards.
    • Charity Initiatives
      • Partner with wildlife conservation organizations.
      • Donate a portion of transaction fees to support gorilla habitats.
  • Phase 4: Growth & Expansion
    • Game Development
      • Develop a simple Harambe-themed game where players earn tokens.
    • Cross-Chain Integration
      • Explore bridging $HARAMBES to other blockchains (e.g., BSC, Polygon) to grow the community.
